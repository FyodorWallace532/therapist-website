Success without meaning is its own kind of suffering.

You've done what you were supposed to do. You built the career, supported the family, hit the milestones. But instead of fulfillment, you feel a quiet emptiness. A restlessness that won't go away. A question that keeps surfacing: "Is this really it?"

This isn't a midlife crisis. This is your soul telling you it's time to go deeper.

This work is for men who are:

  • Successful by external standards but feel unfulfilled internally
  • Questioning their identity beyond their career, title, or role as provider
  • Navigating a major transition — retirement, empty nest, career change, divorce
  • Feeling disconnected from their values or unsure what they even value anymore
  • Searching for a sense of purpose that goes beyond obligation and achievement
  • Wanting to live more authentically but unsure what that even looks like

What this looks like in practice

This isn't about finding a new hobby or making a vision board. Purpose and meaning work goes to the core of who you are — your values, your wounds, your unlived life. We'll explore the stories you've been told about what it means to be a man and which of those stories are still serving you.

Drawing from existential therapy, Jungian depth psychology, and relational approaches, we'll work together to help you reconnect with what genuinely matters — and build a life that reflects it.

This work can happen as part of individual therapy sessions or as a focused engagement over a set number of sessions.
