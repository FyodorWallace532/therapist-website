Welcome

I'm glad you're here.

I know that reaching out for help — especially as a man — can feel like one of the hardest things you've ever done. Our culture tells men to be strong, to have it all figured out, to never show weakness. But the truth is, it takes real strength to look honestly at your life and say, "Something needs to change."

In my work with men, I create a space that's direct, honest, and free of judgment. Together, we'll dig into what's really going on beneath the surface — in your marriage, in your sense of self, and in the meaning you're building (or searching for) in your life. This isn't about fixing what's "broken." It's about becoming who you're meant to be.
