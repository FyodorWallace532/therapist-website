How I Can Help

Individual Therapy

Therapy for Men

Feeling stuck, overwhelmed, or disconnected from yourself? You're not alone — and you don't have to white-knuckle your way through it. Individual therapy gives you a private, judgment-free space to explore the thoughts, feelings, and patterns that have been running your life on autopilot.

Together, we'll work on understanding what's really driving your frustration, your anxiety, or that nagging emptiness — and build a path toward something better.

Learn More

Couples Therapy

Marriage & Relationship Work

Every couple hits rough patches. But when the disconnection becomes the norm — when conversations turn into arguments, silence replaces intimacy, and you feel more like roommates than partners — it's time to get help.

I help couples (and men navigating relationship challenges) learn to communicate honestly, rebuild trust, and reconnect on a level that actually matters.

Learn More

Purpose & Meaning

Men's Purpose & Meaning Work

You've checked the boxes — career, house, family — but something still feels hollow. The question "Is this all there is?" keeps surfacing, and no amount of hustle or distraction makes it go away.

This work is about reconnecting with what actually matters to you — not what you were told should matter. We'll explore your values, your identity beyond your roles, and what a meaningful life looks like on your terms.
